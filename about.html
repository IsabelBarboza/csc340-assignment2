About this website

My name is Isabel Barboza, I am 26 years old, and I have been a Dota 2 fan since around 2016. A high school friend introduced me to the game, and it quickly became my favorite.

Even though my rank medal is not very high, I am very passionate about Dota 2. I enjoy learning from every match, improving my skills, and understanding the strategy behind the game.

I created this website as part of my Software Engineering course at UNCG. While building this site, I practiced writing HTML structure, styling with CSS, and thinking about layout and design. This project helps me improve my technical skills while working on a topic that motivates me.

I chose a dark and bold design with strong visual elements because I wanted the site to feel powerful and immersive, similar to the atmosphere of Dota 2. The colors and style reflect intensity, focus, and strong character identity.

This website represents both who I am as a gamer and my growth as a software development student.
