Phantom Assassin

Universe: Dota 2

Role: Assassin / Carry

Abilities: Stifling Dagger, Coup de Grace, Phantom Strike and Blur

Backstory: Mortred, known as Phantom Assassin, is a deadly killer trained by the secretive order of the Veiled Sisters. She strikes from the shadows, delivering devastating critical hits to her enemies and disappearing before they can react.

Personality: Quiet and mysterious, Mortred trusts very few people. She is careful, smart, and always acts fast in battle, which makes her very dangerous to anyone who faces her.

Strengths: High critical damage, excellent at quickly eliminating enemies

Quote: "Swift as the shadows." / "I go unseen."
